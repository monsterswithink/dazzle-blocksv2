About us
--------

JS SDK to add powerful collaborative features into your product ridiculously fast! Popular features include: Figma-style comments, Notifications, Loom-style recording, Slack-style huddle, Webflow-style single editor mode and more. Some popular categories of apps that use us: * Creative tools: Video editing, website builders, where users want to review and collaborate on the designs, videos etc; * Analytics tools: Users want to discuss data together; * Project management tools: Users want to discuss progress, provide more insights on tasks inside the app itself and many more. What Stripe to Payments, Velt SDK is doing that to Collaboration. We‚Äôre a team of ex-Googlers. We've launched products like Augmented Reality in Google Search & Maps. Backed by Y Combinator.
